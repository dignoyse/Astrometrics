Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our solar system.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and has a thick, toxic atmosphere.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only one known to support life.

Mars

Mars, known as the Red Planet, is the fourth planet from the Sun.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and has a Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is famous for its rings.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and has a unique sideways rotation.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun and is known for its intense blue color.

Pluto

Pluto, once considered the ninth planet, is now classified as a dwarf planet.

Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and the fifth largest moon in the solar system.
